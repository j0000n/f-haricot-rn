# Countries by Region

## Africa

### Northern Africa

#### Algeria
- **Culinary Regions:**
  - **Coastal Region:** Known for seafood dishes and Mediterranean influences
  - **Atlas Mountains:** Features tagines and couscous
  - **Sahara Region:** Known for dates and traditional Berber dishes

#### Egypt
- **Culinary Regions:**
  - **Cairo:** Known for koshari (rice, lentils, pasta mix) and fattah
  - **Alexandria:** Famous for seafood specialties like sayadeya
  - **Upper Egypt:** Features traditional dishes like molokhia and ful medames

#### Libya
- **Culinary Regions:**
  - **Tripolitania:** Known for bazin (barley bread) and shorba (soup)
  - **Cyrenaica:** Features Mediterranean-influenced dishes
  - **Fezzan:** Traditional desert cuisine

#### Morocco
- **Culinary Regions:**
  - **Marrakech:** Renowned for tagines and couscous
  - **Fez:** Known for pastilla (sweet and savory pie) and harira soup
  - **Casablanca:** Coastal cuisine with seafood specialties
  - **Atlas Mountains:** Berber cuisine with preserved meats

#### Sudan
- **Culinary Regions:**
  - **Northern Sudan:** Known for ful medames and kisra (sorghum bread)
  - **Central Sudan:** Features aseeda (porridge) and traditional stews
  - **Eastern Sudan:** Coastal influences with fish dishes

#### Tunisia
- **Culinary Regions:**
  - **Tunis:** Known for brik (fried pastry) and harissa
  - **Sfax:** Famous for seafood and fish couscous
  - **Djerba:** Island cuisine with Mediterranean influences

### East Asia

#### China
- **Culinary Regions:**
  - **Eight Great Traditions:**
    - **Guangdong (Cantonese):** Known for dim sum, roast duck, and steamed dishes. Emphasizes fresh ingredients with minimal seasoning
    - **Sichuan:** Famous for bold, spicy flavors with Sichuan peppercorns. Signature dishes: Kung Pao chicken, Mapo tofu, hot pot
    - **Anhui:** Features wild herbs and simple preparation methods. Known for braised dishes
    - **Shandong:** Seafood dishes and rich flavors. Known for sweet and sour dishes
    - **Fujian:** Emphasizes umami taste with seafood and soups. Signature dish: Buddha Jumps Over the Wall
    - **Jiangsu:** Refined cooking techniques with sweet and light flavors. Known for Huaiyang cuisine
    - **Hunan:** Spicy and sour flavors with smoked and cured meats. Known for steamed fish head with chopped chili
    - **Zhejiang:** Fresh and light flavors, often incorporating seafood. Known for Dongpo pork
  - **Other Regional Cuisines:**
    - **Northeastern (Dongbei):** Hearty dishes with pickled vegetables
    - **Beijing:** Imperial cuisine, Peking duck, and Beijing hot pot
    - **Shanghai:** Sweet and savory dishes, xiaolongbao (soup dumplings)
    - **Yunnan:** Diverse ethnic cuisines with mushrooms and flowers
    - **Xinjiang:** Uyghur cuisine with lamb and naan bread
    - **Tibetan:** High-altitude cuisine with yak meat and tsampa
    - **Hakka:** Preserved foods and hearty dishes
