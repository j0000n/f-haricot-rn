- **Recipe Creators:**
    
    - _CookingWithAlia (Alia Al-Kasimi)_ – A Moroccan-born chef and YouTuber often featuring Maghreb cuisine.     - **North India:**
        
        - **Punjab:** Known for butter chicken, tandoori dishes, sarson da saag with makki di roti
            
        - **Delhi:** Mughlai cuisine, street food, chaat
            
        - **Uttar Pradesh:** Awadhi cuisine, kebabs, biryani
            
        - **Rajasthan:** Dal baati churma, laal maas (spicy mutton)
            
        - **Himachal Pradesh:** Mountain cuisine with red rice
            
    - **South India:**
        
        - **Tamil Nadu:** Known for dosa, idli, sambar, Chettinad cuisine
            
        - **Kerala:** Coconut-based dishes, seafood, appam
            
        - **Karnataka:** Udupi cuisine, bisi bele bath, Mysore pak
            
        - **Andhra Pradesh/Telangana:** Spicy curries, biryani, pickles
            
    - **East India:**
        
        - **West Bengal:** Known for fish curry (macher jhol), sweets (rasgulla, sandesh), mishti doi
            
        - **Odisha:** Pakhala, chhena poda, seafood
            
        - **Bihar:** Litti chokha, sattu
            
        - **Jharkhand:** Traditional tribal cuisine
            
    - **West India:**
        
        - **Gujarat:** Known for dhokla, thepla, fafda, vegetarian thali
            
        - **Maharashtra:** Vada pav, misal pav, puran poli, Kolhapuri cuisine
            
        - **Goa:** Portuguese-influenced cuisine, vindaloo, fish curry
            
    - **Northeast India:**
        
        - **Assam:** Known for fish tenga (sour fish curry), bamboo shoots
            
        - **Manipur:** Eromba, kangshoi
            
        - **Nagaland:** Smoked meats, bamboo shoots, axone (fermented soybean)
            
        - **Mizoram:** Bai, sawhchiar
            
- **Recipe Creators:**
    
    - _Sanjeev Kapoor_ – India’s most influential celebrity chef and a passionate ambassador for Indian cuisine[foodandwine.com](https://www.foodandwine.com/author/sanjeev-kapoor#:~:text=Sanjeev%20Kapoor%20is%20arguably%20India%E2%80%99s,popular%20chain%20The%20Yellow%20Chilli). He owns a YouTube channel “Sanjeev Kapoor Khazana” with over 7 million subscribers, where he presents tried-and-tested recipes and cooking tips, making Indian cooking accessible globally.     - _Tarla Dalal (late)_ – One of India’s first cookbook authors and TV chefs, Tarla’s legacy continues via her website and YouTube channel. Her vegetarian recipes (Gujarati, Maharashtrian, etc.) are systematically documented – from simple dhokla to elaborate thalis – and remain a go-to resource[bloggers.feedspot.com](https://bloggers.feedspot.com/italian_food_blogs/#:~:text=FeedSpot%20bloggers,Ciao%20Italia%20Blog%20%C2%B7%208). Platforms: Website (Indian, native).
        
    - _Nisha Madhulika_ – A Hindi-language YouTube star (housewife-turned-chef) focusing on pure vegetarian home recipes. Platforms: YouTube (Indian, native).

    - _Ranveer Brar_ – A celebrity chef from Lucknow, India, who runs a popular Hindi-English YouTube channel.     - _The Curry Guy (Dan Toombs)_ – A British food blogger passionate about Indian curry techniques[reddit.com](https://www.reddit.com/r/IndianFood/comments/dqp9kh/best_youtube_channel_for_authentic_indian_cooking/#:~:text=Best%20YouTube%20channel%20for%20authentic,1).
